Management Information System is the application of information systems within the Organization to support information - information needed by all levels of management. Management information system is a system that includes the process of collecting, managing, storing up to the process of delivering data and information about staffing in a work unit. Employee data storage is the main thing in staffing issues, especially in finding or changing employee data to then be adjusted to the needs. The application of the Personnel Information System is made by the type of case study research at PMI, where data collection techniques used include observation, interviews, and literature studies. For the analysis and design methods used in making the system using ERD as a data model and DFD as a process model.

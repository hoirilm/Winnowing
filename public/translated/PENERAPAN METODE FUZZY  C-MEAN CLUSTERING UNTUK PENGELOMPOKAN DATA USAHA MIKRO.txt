In the Office of Cooperatives and SMEs Bangkalan district has a lot of data including asset data, turnover data, the number of workers, the type of business activity, the name of the business owner, and the year the business was established with different values. From this problem to get the results of grouping the right microbusiness data, it is necessary to have a clustering using the Fuzzy C-Means method, the purpose of this study is to classify microbusiness data based on asset data, turnover and the number of workers. 300 micro business data. The results of the study indicate that the clustering based on the Fuzzy C-Means method gets 8 clusters with a sillhoutte value of 0.4314

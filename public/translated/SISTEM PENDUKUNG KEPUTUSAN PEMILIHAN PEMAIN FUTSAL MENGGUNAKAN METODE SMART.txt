Many Futsal Schools make selections for young players who practice futsal at the school. But, the conventional method of skill and physical selection makes this coaching less optimal. One problem that arises is the selection of players who will be included in the competition. Many matches require physical conditions and the capabilities of these players. Then the coach must be accurate in seeing the potential of the players, so that the school can win the match. Based on the above problems, there needs to be a decision support system for the selection of futsal players. Based on the players' test results, this system gets the basic abilities of the players. The best Sisten player has a similarity accuracy of 31.25% with the choice of the coach. This system is expected to make it easier for coaches to determine their best players and optimize their abilities well.

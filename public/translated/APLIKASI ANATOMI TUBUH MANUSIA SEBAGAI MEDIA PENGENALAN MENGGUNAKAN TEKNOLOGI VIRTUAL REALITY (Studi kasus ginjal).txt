Virtual reality (VR) is a simulation technology from the real world that is made exactly into virtual form using Android. In virtual reality technology displays 3D objects, sound, and text. Virtual reality is widely used in various fields, one of which is used in education. In the field of education, virtual reality technology is used as a more interesting, creative and innovative learning media. Virtual reality (VR) technology is used in learning the anatomy of the human body. In this application there are features, namely zooming and simulating the workings of the kidneys contained in the formation mechanism of urine. User experience indicators with an average value of 81%, MGBL indicators with an average value of 74%, and Evaluation Heuristic indicators with an average value of 83%. Based on the average results of each indicator, it is concluded that the application is easy to use for teaching and learning.

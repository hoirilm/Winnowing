At this time, credit is in great demand by the community because by submitting a request for credit, the community can open a business or develop their business to be more advanced. BPR UMKM Bank itself is engaged in the business credit sector in which the credit lending decision system still uses manual calculations where the risk of non-performing loans is still high. Therefore we need a credit worthiness decision support system at the BPR UMKM Bank to find out how fast the process is carried out by the bank to conduct credit worthiness assessment using this system. In connection with this goal, this study applies Tsukamoto's fuzzy logic method. From the test data as much as 50 data that have been done produce an accuracy rate of 90%
